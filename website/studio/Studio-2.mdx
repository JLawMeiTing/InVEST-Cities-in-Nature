:::tip About the Presentation

In this studio, your team and yourself are to present a clear understanding about your allocated district. Problem(s) of the district and key concern(s) of the various stakeholders within the teams should be highlighted. The presentation will be 10 minutes for each team, with another 10 minutes of feedback.

:::

:::info Submission Guide

Deadline: **Sunday 11:59 pm, 26th February 2023** (Note: Late submissions will not be accepted).

You must submit your presentation slides to [Canvas](https://canvas.nus.edu.sg/courses/42112/assignments/22959). Please name your file name in this standard format: "TX, GY", with "X" being your tutorial group number and "Y" being your team number. 

:::
